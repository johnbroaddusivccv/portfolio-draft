Cognitive Computing

The Programming Language Julia

Cognitive computing is the use of computerized models to simulate the human thought process in complex situations where the answers may be ambiguous and uncertain. Not to be confused with Artificial Intelligence, the theory and development of computer systems able to perform tasks that normally require human intelligence, Cognitive Computing with the programming langauge Julia is well suited for numerical analysis and computational sciences. Although Julia is a general purpose programming language (any application can be built in it.) Julia is a high level, high performance, dynamic programming language that is by far the best exhibition of my tech life skills.

Search Engine Optimization

SEO

Google's mission statement and primary purpose is to organize the world's data. It is what most people use when inquiring about anything. Having an in depth understanding of the search optimizations algorithm is complex, anti-pattern, and takes a lot of self study and investigation to target just how we can climb the ranks of SEO. Using Keyword software we have a better understanding of the queries that can be manifested from words relating to our business, product, and service. Using keyword software we can blacklist our competitors and create a CSV file full of queries and keywords. Using Unique Images that exist nowhere else in our web pages promotes better SEO. Utilizing image alt tags and double quotes in said alt tags tells the search function "I AM IMPORTANT." Using related keywords within the first and last 100 words of our webpages has shown my clients successful results. Using related keywords in h1, h2, h3 tags has also proven successful. The readablity of a webpage can also be a factor in SEO. Their is no better promotion for SEO than having a wonderful business, product, and service. There is no blueprint for this but working on your website and application's functionality is a great place to start.
