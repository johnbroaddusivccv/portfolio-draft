Sequence of Steps for Action

The first step in executing any digital operation is understanding the problem and then mapping out how the problem can be solved. I often draw a map by hand or create a chart to better understand the issue.

1

Identify The Issue(s)

Can we make your website and application better? What is the best technology to use for your website or application? Does it look and behave professionally?

2

Understand The Issue(s)

This can be an issue inside or outside of the codebase, text-editor, IED, technologies utilized, or other forms of conflict. When I focus on the solution, the solution expands.

3

Isolate The Issue(s)

The enviroment in which an issue survives should be minized to the greatest extent of our abilities. After knowing and isolating the issue we can begin to eliminate it.

4

Decimation of Issue(s)

The clear understanding and isolation of all issues makes whatever you want your website or application to accomplish possible.
